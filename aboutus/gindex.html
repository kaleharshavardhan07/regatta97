About Regatta

Regatta is an annual boat festival organized by the students of the COEP Technological University, Pune. The festival, which has a history of 97 years, is a major attraction for the people of Pune and every year draws a colossal and immense amount of crowd. The word "Regatta" signifies a series of boat races. Every year, the Regatta features more than 165 distinctive boats. It reaches new levels of creativity and talent. Since 1928, the Regatta has been a prestigious COEP tradition. It is the stunning result of diligent practice, astute brains, devotion, and fervent determination. The COEP Regatta is a magnificent competition that showcases the tenacity and talent of the students who train day and night to dazzle a crowd of over 12000 spectators with their astounding diversity of boats. Now looking forward we are all set to host our 96th edition with a lot of enthusiasm.
