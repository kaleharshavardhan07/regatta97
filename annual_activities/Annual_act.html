MINI REGATTA

Celebrating the joy of setting sail at our Mini Regatta! To introduce with you our annual event, we organised a glimpse of it. Mini Regatta sets just a trailer of Regatta. We proudly inaugurate this nautical spectacle, where every ripple signifies new beginnings.

MOONLIGHT PUNTING

Moonlight Punting is an event held on the auspicious occasion of Kojagiri Purnima on the bank of river Mula. A night of punting magic where everyone gets an opportunity to sail into serenity under the silver moonlight. Special refreshments were provided to all participants.

DEEPOTSAV

Deepotsav : an event that took place on the auspicious occasion of Diwali🪔 We all celebrated together this festival of lights with sparkling moments, shimmering diyas, and endless happiness.✨ Boat Club was enlightened with beautiful lights and rangolis. "Deepotsav: Where tradition meets radiance."

SURVIVAL OF THE FITTEST

As the things were getting back to normal, 96th REGATTA organized an Offline workout challenge event- Survival of the Fittest by taking all the precautions As it is rightly said, "To keep the body in good health is a duty… otherwise we shall not be able to keep our mind strong and clear!!"

TREASURE HUNT

“Treasure hunt” one of the most awaited event was organized for first-year students second-year students . The main aim of the event was to drift the students away from their academic routine.

ALUMNI RACES
